How We Worked Together

The project was split into two clear tracks: one developer owned the detection pipeline (MediaPipe integration, OpenCV rendering, webcam management, and bug fixes), while the other owned the classification and presentation layer (angle thresholds, website, and Flask backend). Both tracks were developed in parallel and merged iteratively, with the two sides regularly testing end-to-end.

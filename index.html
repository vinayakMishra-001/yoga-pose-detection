ESP32-CAM Support

Point a wireless ESP32-CAM module at your yoga mat. Flask reads the MJPEG stream, annotates frames with MediaPipe skeleton overlays, and streams the result back to the browser — no USB cable needed.

How It Works

01
📷
Capture Input
Webcam or ESP32-CAM — all processed by the Flask backend via OpenCV.
02
🦴
Detect Landmarks
MediaPipe Pose identifies 33 key body landmarks in 3D space with confidence scores.
03
📐
Calculate Angles
Joint angles (shoulder, elbow, knee) are computed using arctangent mathematics.
04
✅
Classify Pose
Angle thresholds determine which of 6 poses matches and display a label on screen.
